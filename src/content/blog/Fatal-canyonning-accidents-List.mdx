The reason behind this dark list is two-fold. Firstly, I want to honor and remember those who shared our passion and to preserve their names for as long as I live. Secondly, and perhaps more importantly, I want to show that this is an extreme sport that demands preparation and respect for the streams.

# Spain (2024)

**35 m. fall from a rappel**

Javier Braojos, a 47-year-old spanish policeman passed away after falling from a rappel.

Aragon Pyrenees Spain\
**When**: 09/05/2024\
**Possible reason**: Unknown.\
**Where**: [Barranco Sía](https://www.docuwiki.infobarrancos.es/doku.php?id=barrancos:huesca:sia)\
**Local news**: [Heraldo de Aragon](https://www.heraldo.es/noticias/aragon/huesca/2024/05/10/muere-otro-barranquista-al-caer-en-un-rapel-en-yesero-1732593.html)

**Drowned**

Miguel Ángel Hernández Andrés, a 35-year-old spanish firefighter passed drowned in a sensible rappel.

Aragon Pyrenees Spain\
**When**: 30/04/2024\
**Possible reason**: High (yet not impossible) flow. Alone.\
**Personal hypotesis**: 90-meter rappel split into 2x45m with a water pool in between. With high flow, the waterfall can push you into the rock. There is a rock in the water pool where ropes can get entangled to.\
**Where**: [Barranco de Sorrosal](https://www.docuwiki.infobarrancos.es/doku.php?id=barrancos:huesca:barranco_del_sorrosal)\
**Local news**: [Heraldo de Aragon](https://www.heraldo.es/noticias/aragon/huesca/2024/05/10/muere-otro-barranquista-al-caer-en-un-rapel-en-yesero-1732593.html)\
[IG video from rescue](https://www.instagram.com/reel/C6br8PiIHP6)

**Stuck in stones**

A 60-year-old spanish woman from Castilleja de la Cuesta (Sevilla) died drowned.

Jaen Spain\
**When**: 15/04/2024\
**Possible reason**: She got stuck between two stones at the chest. A waster stream was passing
over her. Died drowned.\
**Where**: [Barranco de la Bolera](https://www.docuwiki.infobarrancos.es/doku.php?id=barrancos:jaen:bolera)\
**Local news**: [Lo que pasa en Pozo Alcón](https://loquepasaenpozoalcon.es/muere-una-mujer-al-practicar-barranquismo-en-el-guadalentin/)\
[Cadena Ser](https://cadenaser.com/andalucia/2024/04/16/asi-fue-el-arriesgado-rescate-del-cuerpo-de-la-mujer-fallecida-en-pozo-alcon-mientras-hacia-barranquismo-radio-jaen/)

# Spain (2023)

**Leg stuck when rappeling**

Roberto Paz Diz 35-year-old spanish man from A Illa and settled in Valencia passed away after getting stuck when rappeling.

Granada Spain Sierra Nevada\
**When**: 19/11/2023\
**Possible reason**: Leg stuck while rappeling. Died drowned.\
**Where**: [Barranco del río Trévelez](https://www.docuwiki.infobarrancos.es/doku.php?id=barrancos:granada:trevelez)\
**Local news**: [Faro de Vigo](https://www.farodevigo.es/arousa/2023/11/21/muere-joven-illa-barranquismo-granada-94923581.html)

**Couple died drowned**

Carlos Iván Iglesias Peña, a 43-year-old spaniard from Burgos and his girlfriend, Beatriz de Cabo González, a 37-year-old spaniard from Pitiegua (Salamanca) passed away.

Spain Asturias
**When**: 13/09/2023\
**Possible reason**: The man was found stuck between two stones. The woman was found upstream, in a waterfall. High flow after intense rain.\
**Where**: [Barranco de Pompedru](https://www.docuwiki.infobarrancos.es/doku.php?id=barrancos:asturias:pompedru)\
**Local news**: [Diario de Burgos](https://www.diariodeburgos.es/noticia/za5657526-e0cf-3e48-8d298430beb23320/202309/muere-un-montanero-burgales-al-hacer-barranquismo-en-asturias)
